# Mai Shayi Strategy & Design Plan

## 1. BRAND FOUNDATION
- **Brand Personality**: Warm, authentic, lively, street-smart.
- **Tone of Voice**: Friendly, relatable, slightly playful, culturally rooted.
- **Target Audience**: Students, urban workers, families, youth looking for authentic flavors.
- **Core Value Proposition**: "The warmth of Northern tradition, refined for the modern lifestyle. Authentic street tea delivered fresh."

## 2. WEBSITE STRUCTURE (SITEMAP)
- **Home**: High-conversion landing page.
- **About Us**: The story of Northern tea culture and our mission.
- **Menu / Products**: Visual catalog of teas, snacks, and breakfast specials.
- **Order / Shop**: Streamlined checkout and WhatsApp integration.
- **Locations**: Interactive map of physical stalls/outlets.
- **Testimonials**: Social proof from the community.
- **Blog / Stories**: "The Shayi Chronicles" - stories about tea culture.
- **Contact**: Quick support and inquiry form.

## 3. HOMEPAGE WIREFRAME (Implemented in App.tsx)
- **Hero**: Emotional headline + clear CTA.
- **Social Proof Bar**: Scrolling marquee of trust signals.
- **Product Highlights**: Best-selling teas and snacks.
- **Story Section**: Cultural connection.
- **How It Works**: 3-step process (Pick -> Brew -> Deliver).
- **Trust Signals**: Hygiene and quality badges.
- **Final CTA**: Bold re-engagement.

## 4. COPYWRITING
- **Headlines**:
  1. "Warmth in Every Sip."
  2. "The Heart of the North, Delivered."
  3. "Your Daily Ritual, Reimagined."
- **CTAs**: "Order Your Tea Now", "Get Your Fix", "Experience the Warmth".
- **Brand Story**: Short narrative about the "Mai Shayi" being the heartbeat of the street.

## 5. UI/UX DESIGN DIRECTION
- **Color Palette**: Amber (#D97706), Cream (#FFFBEB), Deep Brown (#451A03).
- **Typography**: Inter (Sans) for readability, Space Grotesk (Serif/Display) for character.
- **Layout**: Mobile-first, card-based, generous whitespace.
- **Images**: High-quality, authentic photography of tea preparation and happy customers.

## 6. CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION (CRO)
- **Sticky CTA**: "Order Now" button always accessible.
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Floating button for low-friction ordering.
- **Social Proof**: Real-time stats (e.g., "2,500+ tea lovers this month").

## 7. TECHNICAL FEATURES
- **PWA Ready**: Fast loading and offline capabilities.
- **WhatsApp API**: Direct order routing.
- **Local Payments**: Paystack/Flutterwave integration.
- **SEO**: Optimized for "Northern Tea", "Best Breakfast Abuja", etc.

## 8. CONTENT STRATEGY
- **UGC**: Encourage customers to share "Shayi Moments" on Instagram.
- **Blog**: "5 Spices That Make Our Tea Special".

## 9. LAUNCH STRATEGY
- **Pre-launch**: "The First Sip" waitlist.
- **Launch Offer**: Buy 1 Get 1 Free for the first 100 orders.
- **Influencer**: Partner with local food bloggers for "Street to Screen" reviews.
